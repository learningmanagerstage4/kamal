COOP Fix Bypass — Adobe Acrobat OAuth Hijacking

CVE: OAuth Flow Hijacking via COOP unsafe-none override
The Bypass
Fix sets COOP: same-origin-allow-popups on all pages.
But any URL with ?code= overrides it to COOP: unsafe-none — restoring full attacker window control.

Protected: acrobat.adobe.com/link/home/ → COOP: same-origin-allow-popups
Bypassed: acrobat.adobe.com/link/home/recent/?code=x → COOP: unsafe-none

2. Click Sign In on the Adobe page
3. Click Continue with Google
4. Watch the log below — attacker auto-hijacks the OAuth
